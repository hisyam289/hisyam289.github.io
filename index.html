Here are some tips to help you stay safer in the future

Tip: #1

Check the URL Carefully

Always hover over links before clicking and ensure the URL looks legitimate. Look out for subtle misspellings or unusual domain extensions.

Tip: #2

Be Suspicious

Never open email attachments or links unless you're expecting them, even if the email seems to come from someone you know. Phishing often uses infected files to install malware.

Tip: #3

Verify the Sender

If you're unsure about an email, verify the sender by contacting them through official channels (don't use the contact information from the email).
